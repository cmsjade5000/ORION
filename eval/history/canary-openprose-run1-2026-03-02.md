# Canary Run 1

**Brief: Weekly Skill Review Enhancement**

**Objective:** Elevate the quality and accelerate the tempo of the weekly skill review process.

**Inputs:**
*   Current review documentation.
*   Aggregated feedback on quality/speed issues.
*   List of skills under review.

**Method:**
1.  **Diagnostic:** Map current workflow, pinpoint bottlenecks, and gather qualitative/quantitative feedback.
2.  **Optimization:** Identify opportunities for automation (scripts/tools) and standardize review criteria for consistency.
3.  **Implementation:** Roll out refined processes, new tools, and updated guidelines. Pilot testing for validation.
4.  **Enablement:** Train reviewers on new procedures and tools.
5.  **Iterate:** Monitor performance metrics and feedback loops for continuous improvement.

**Risks:**
*   Change resistance.
*   Tool integration complexity/failure.
*   Subjectivity in quality measurement.
*   Resource constraints.

**Success Criteria:**
*   20% reduction in average review cycle time.
*   15% increase in reviewer satisfaction.
*   Measurable improvement in review consistency.
*   Successful adoption of at least one major workflow enhancement.

**Next Action:**
*   Delegate workflow analysis and feedback collection to NODE.
*   Schedule stakeholder kick-off to define scope and priorities.
